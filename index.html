Anything But Ordinary.




Anything But Ordinary Productions is a theatrical production company based in the Greater Philadelphia Area. Founded by then-high schoolers, Anything But Ordinary (or ABO for short) was a way for students to create their own productions without the limitations that schools placed on them. Completely independent from any other institution, ABO mounted its first production in 2014, proving to be a massive success. Moving forward, the board of the company decided to take on the ambitious project of creating an exclusive, in-house production. In the summer of 2015, “Where’s Waldo? The Musical” premiered to sold-out crowds and overwhelmingly positive reviews. As the board moves away from high school and into college and beyond, the company has shifted its views from just Philadelphia to New York, and branched out to film by creating “A Jay-Walk on the Wild side”, premiering soon. Checkout the “about” tab for more on our founders and current board members. For more on past and future productions, head over to our “productions” tab. If you have any questions, feel free to get in contact with us, and we’ll get back to you shortly! We promise our productions, and your experience with us will be above and beyond the ordinary…after all, it’s in the name!
