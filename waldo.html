Watch Where's Waldo: The Musical

In 2011, Billy Ambrose, Nicholas Goodyear, and Trey Hennessy had a dream to create a musical based on the universe of Where's Waldo. They sought to create a comedic back story as to why people are searching for Waldo. It was just an idea then.

Four lears later in 2015, after a wonderful experience with A Very Potter Musical with ABO Productions, Billy and Nick knew they had found an amazing group of dedicated individuals - a group that would try anything. In the blink of an eye, Billy and Nick became eager to pick up Where's Waldo?: The Musical again. They began writing.

It wasn't until October of 2014 that Billy and Nick recruited Ben McLaughlin and Sal Azzara to work on the musical. Sal and Nick began exchanging music ideas while Ben, Billy, and Nick dedicated over six hours per week sitting in Panera and pouring their brains into a Google Doc that contained Where's Waldo?: The Musical.

After over at least $1000 spent at Panera, many late nights of writing, scrapping countless songs, arguments, hurt feelings, a brutally difficult casting with the help of Nicole Telesco and Andrew Cohen, and a month and a half of hard work, the cast, crew, writers and directors have put together an incredible show.


Where's Waldo?: The Musical will be performed on July 23, 24, and 26 at the Plumsteadville Volunteer Fire Company Banquet Hall. Rather than tickets, there is a recommended $10 donation at the door. The show is PG-13. Everyone involved in the production wishes to see you there!
